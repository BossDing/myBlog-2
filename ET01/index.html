英语很重要 一点都没错

DayOne

早上六点半起来学一点英语表达：
1.

You’ve been good to me my whole life.

2.

Sentence to be carried out immediately.

3.

‘Trust me’ means absolutely zero inside those walls.

4.

Take additional time to consider your response.
